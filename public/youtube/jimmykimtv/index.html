Edited over 27 YouTube videos for Jimmy Kim TV in 2025, spanning travel vlogs, food reviews, and experience-based content.

The editing workflow was centered in Adobe Premiere Pro, making use of licensed stock footage and sound libraries, with selective application of third-party plugins to refine pacing, transitions, and overall polish.

Light motion graphics and minimal animations were produced in After Effects when needed, supporting the narrative without distracting from the vlog-driven, authentic tone of the channel.

The work emphasized dynamic pacing, clear narrative flow, and a balance between authenticity and entertainment, while maintaining consistent visual and tonal alignment with the channel’s style and audience.

The videos edited during this period collectively surpassed 4.53 million views within one year, reflecting strong audience engagement and sustained performance on YouTube.
