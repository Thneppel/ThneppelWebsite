Direction, content production, and video editing for the Nice N Raw YouTube channel, with responsibilities spanning creative direction, production planning, and post-production.

The production workflow included on-set direction, camera operation, and coordination of audiovisual resources, ensuring visual coherence and a strong narrative foundation across episodes.

Editing was primarily handled in Adobe Premiere Pro, with extensive integration of After Effects for motion graphics, visual enhancements, and animated elements that played a significant role in the channel’s visual identity. Licensed stock footage, sound libraries, and third-party plugins were used to refine pacing, transitions, and overall polish.

Over 30 videos were produced, collectively reaching 71,052 views, reflecting consistent audience engagement and solid performance for a growing YouTube channel.

The work emphasized visual storytelling, dynamic editing, and cohesive narrative flow, reinforcing the channel’s tone, identity, and connection with its audience.
